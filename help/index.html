Booksie Help

This is the help page. Here you can find information about how to use our website and its features.

Getting Started

Just click on the links to get started. You don't need to create an account. Follow the instructions on each page - they are intuitive, really.

Using the Home Page

You can access the home page to view existing books. You can also add books by entering the title, author and other information. All information is stored locally and will not be shared with others.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or issues, please don't hesitate to contact us using the contact form on our website. We'll get back to you as soon as possible.
